## üì± Step 2: WhatsApp Business Connection (10 minutes)

### Link Your WhatsApp Business Account

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="WhatsApp Business API">
    **For Medium to Large Businesses (Recommended)**
    
    **Prerequisites**:
    - Dedicated business phone number
    - WhatsApp Business API access
    - Verified business profile
    
    **Connection Process**:
    1. **Navigate to Integrations** in your Merx dashboard
    2. **Select WhatsApp Business API** connection option
    3. **Enter your phone number** in international format (+1234567890)
    4. **Verify ownership** through SMS or call verification
    5. **Grant permissions** for Merx to manage your WhatsApp Business
    6. **Test connection** by sending a test message
    
    **Verification**: You should see a green "Connected" status in your dashboard
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="WhatsApp Business App">
    **For Small Businesses and Testing**
    
    **Setup Process**:
    1. **Download WhatsApp Business** from App Store or Google Play
    2. **Register with business number** (different from personal WhatsApp)
    3. **Complete business profile** with all required information
    4. **In Merx dashboard**, go to Integrations > WhatsApp Business
    5. **Scan QR code** displayed in Merx with WhatsApp Business app
    6. **Confirm connection** when prompted in WhatsApp Business
    
    **Important**: Keep WhatsApp Business app open and connected to internet
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Troubleshooting">
    **Common Issues and Solutions**
    
    **"Phone number already in use"**:
    - Ensure you're not using your personal WhatsApp number
    - Use a dedicated business line
    - Contact support if you need to transfer a number
    
    **"Verification failed"**:
    - Check phone number format (include country code)
    - Ensure strong internet connection
    - Try verification via call instead of SMS
    
    **"QR code won't scan"**:
    - Refresh the Merx page to generate new QR code
    - Ensure good lighting when scanning
    - Try from a different device or browser
    
    **Still having issues?** Contact our support team for assistance
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## üë• Step 4: Customer Data Import (15 minutes)

### Import Your Existing Customers

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="CSV Upload">
    **Upload your customer database**
    
    **Prepare Your CSV File**:
    
    **Required Columns**:
    - **Name**: Customer's full name
    - **Phone**: International format (+1234567890)
    
    **Optional Columns**:
    - **Email**: Customer email address
    - **Company**: Business name (for B2B)
    - **Location**: City, state, or country
    - **Tags**: Custom labels (VIP, New Customer, etc.)
    - **Notes**: Additional customer information
    - **Last Purchase**: Date of last purchase
    - **Total Spent**: Customer lifetime value
    
    **Upload Process**:
    1. **Go to Contacts** > Import Contacts
    2. **Select CSV file** from your computer
    3. **Map columns** to Merx fields
    4. **Review data** for accuracy
    5. **Start import** and wait for completion
    
    **Validation**: Merx will check phone number formats and flag any issues
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="CRM Integration">
    **Sync with your existing CRM system**
    
    **Supported CRM Platforms**:
    - **Salesforce**: Enterprise CRM integration
    - **HubSpot**: Inbound marketing and sales platform
    - **Pipedrive**: Sales-focused CRM
    - **Zoho CRM**: All-in-one business suite
    - **Monday.com**: Project and customer management
    - **Airtable**: Flexible database platform
    
    **Integration Setup**:
    1. **Navigate to Integrations** in Merx dashboard
    2. **Select your CRM** from available options
    3. **Authenticate** with your CRM credentials
    4. **Choose data to sync**:
       - Contact information
       - Deal/opportunity data
       - Interaction history
       - Custom fields
    5. **Set sync frequency** (real-time, hourly, daily)
    6. **Test synchronization** with a small dataset
    
    **Benefits**: Automatic two-way sync keeps data current in both systems
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Manual Entry">
    **Add contacts individually**
    
    **When to Use Manual Entry**:
    - Small customer base (under 50 contacts)
    - VIP customers requiring special attention
    - Testing with select customers first
    - Adding new contacts one by one
    
    **Manual Entry Process**:
    1. **Go to Contacts** > Add New Contact
    2. **Enter required information**:
       - Full name
       - Phone number (international format)
       - Email address (optional)
    3. **Add custom information**:
       - Tags for organization
       - Notes about customer
       - Preferences and interests
    4. **Set communication preferences**:
       - Preferred contact times
       - Language preference
       - Opt-in status confirmation
    5. **Save contact** and repeat for others
    
    **Best Practice**: Start with your most engaged customers for initial testing
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Warning>
**Compliance Important**: Only import contacts who have given explicit permission to receive WhatsApp messages from your business. This ensures compliance with WhatsApp policies and maintains your sender reputation.
</Warning>
