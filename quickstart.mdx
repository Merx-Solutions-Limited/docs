## ðŸ“± Step 2: Connect WhatsApp Business (10 minutes)

### Link Your WhatsApp Business Account

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="WhatsApp Business API">
    **For Businesses (Recommended)**
    
    1. **Get Your Phone Number Verified**
       - Use a dedicated business phone number
       - This will be your customers' contact point
       - Can't be the same as your personal WhatsApp
    
    2. **Connect via QR Code**
       - Open WhatsApp Business on your phone
       - Go to Settings > Business Tools > WhatsApp Business API
       - Scan the QR code shown in Merx dashboard
    
    3. **Verify Connection**
       - Send a test message to confirm everything works
       - You should see the message appear in your Merx dashboard
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="WhatsApp Business App">
    **For Small Businesses**
    
    1. **Download WhatsApp Business**
       - Available on App Store and Google Play
       - Use your business phone number to register
    
    2. **Complete Business Profile**
       - Add your business name and description
       - Upload your logo and business hours
       - Add your website and location
    
    3. **Connect to Merx**
       - Use the integration wizard in your Merx dashboard
       - Follow the step-by-step connection process
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Need Help?">
    **Common Issues & Solutions**
    
    - **Phone number already in use**: Make sure you're not using your personal WhatsApp number
    - **Verification failed**: Check that your phone has good internet connection
    - **Can't scan QR code**: Try refreshing the page and scanning again
    - **Still stuck?**: Contact our support team - we're here to help!
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## ðŸ“ž Step 4: Import Your Contacts (5 minutes)

### Add Your Customer List

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="CSV Upload">
    **Upload Your Existing Contacts**
    
    1. **Prepare Your File**
       - Export contacts from your current system
       - Required columns: Name, Phone Number
       - Optional: Email, Company, Tags
    
    2. **Upload to Merx**
       - Go to Contacts > Import
       - Select your CSV file
       - Map columns to Merx fields
    
    3. **Review and Confirm**
       - Check that phone numbers are formatted correctly
       - Verify contact information looks accurate
       - Click "Import" to add contacts
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="CRM Integration">
    **Sync with Your Existing CRM**
    
    **Supported Platforms**:
    - Salesforce
    - HubSpot
    - Pipedrive
    - Zoho CRM
    - Google Contacts
    
    **Setup Process**:
    1. Go to Integrations in your dashboard
    2. Select your CRM platform
    3. Follow the authentication steps
    4. Choose which contacts to sync
    5. Enable automatic synchronization
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Manual Entry">
    **Add Contacts One by One**
    
    **When to Use**:
    - Small contact list (under 50 contacts)
    - VIP customers you want to add personally
    - Testing with a few contacts first
    
    **How to Add**:
    1. Go to Contacts > Add New
    2. Enter contact information
    3. Add relevant tags and notes
    4. Save and repeat for other contacts
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Warning>
**Important**: Only import contacts who have given you permission to message them on WhatsApp. This ensures compliance with WhatsApp's policies and maintains your sender reputation.
</Warning>
