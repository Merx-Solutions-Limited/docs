### Message Best Practices

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Writing Guidelines">
    **Create compelling content that converts**
    
    **Structure**:
    1. **Hook**: Grab attention in the first line
    2. **Value**: Clearly state what's in it for them
    3. **Action**: Include a clear call-to-action
    4. **Urgency**: Create appropriate urgency when relevant
    
    **Tone Guidelines**:
    - **Conversational**: Write like you're talking to a friend
    - **Authentic**: Use your brand's natural voice
    - **Helpful**: Focus on customer benefit, not just selling
    - **Respectful**: Honor their time and attention
    
    **Length**: Keep messages concise (under 160 characters when possible)
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Visual Design">
    **Make your messages visually appealing**
    
    **Image Guidelines**:
    - Use high-quality, relevant images
    - Optimize for mobile viewing
    - Include your branding consistently
    - Test different visual styles
    
    **Video Best Practices**:
    - Keep videos under 30 seconds
    - Add captions for accessibility
    - Start with an engaging hook
    - Include clear call-to-action
    
    **Brand Consistency**:
    - Use consistent colors and fonts
    - Include logo when appropriate
    - Maintain visual style across campaigns
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Timing Optimization">
    **Send messages when customers are most likely to engage**
    
    **Best Times** (General Guidelines):
    - **Weekdays**: 10 AM - 12 PM, 2 PM - 4 PM
    - **Weekends**: 10 AM - 2 PM
    - **Avoid**: Early morning (before 9 AM), late evening (after 8 PM)
    
    **Industry Variations**:
    - **B2B**: Tuesday-Thursday, business hours
    - **Retail**: Weekends and evenings perform well
    - **Restaurants**: Lunch (11 AM-1 PM) and dinner (5 PM-7 PM) times
    
    **Personalization**: Use customer timezone and historical engagement data
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## üìä Performance Analytics

### Measure What Matters

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Delivery Metrics" icon="paper-plane">
    **Track message delivery success**
    
    - **Delivery Rate**: Percentage of messages successfully delivered
    - **Delivery Time**: Average time to reach recipients
    - **Failed Deliveries**: Messages that couldn't be delivered
    - **Bounce Rate**: Invalid or inactive phone numbers
    
    **Benchmarks**: 95%+ delivery rate is excellent
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Engagement Metrics" icon="heart">
    **Measure customer interaction**
    
    - **Open Rate**: Percentage of messages opened/read
    - **Response Rate**: Customers who replied to messages
    - **Click-Through Rate**: Clicks on links or buttons
    - **Time to Response**: How quickly customers engage
    
    **Benchmarks**: 90%+ open rate, 20%+ response rate
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Conversion Metrics" icon="target">
    **Track business outcomes**
    
    - **Conversion Rate**: Percentage completing desired action
    - **Revenue Attribution**: Sales generated from campaigns
    - **Cost Per Acquisition**: Campaign cost per new customer
    - **Return on Investment**: Revenue vs. campaign cost
    
    **Benchmarks**: 5-15% conversion rate depending on industry
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
