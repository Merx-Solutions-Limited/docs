    | Customer Message | AI Understanding | Response Type |
    |------------------|------------------|---------------|

    | "Do you have this in blue?" | Product inquiry - color availability | Product search with filters |

  <Tab title="Context Awareness">
    **Remembering the Conversation**
    
    The AI maintains context throughout the entire conversation:
    
    **Customer**: "I'm looking for a dress for my wedding"
    **AI**: "Congratulations! I'd love to help you find the perfect wedding dress. What style are you looking for?"
    
    **Customer**: "Something elegant and simple"
    **AI**: "Perfect! Based on your preference for elegant and simple styles for your wedding, here are some beautiful options..." *(shows relevant products)*
    
    **Customer**: "Do you have the first one in ivory?"
    **AI**: "Yes! The [specific dress name] is available in ivory. Would you like to see how it looks, or do you have questions about sizing?"
    
    <Check>
    **The AI remembers**: It's a wedding dress, customer wants elegant/simple, and which specific dress they're asking about.
    </Check>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Emotional Intelligence">
    **Understanding Customer Emotions**
    
    Our AI detects emotional cues and responds appropriately:
    
    - **Frustrated customers**: Empathetic responses, priority escalation
    - **Excited customers**: Enthusiastic engagement, upselling opportunities
    - **Confused customers**: Patient explanations, step-by-step guidance
    - **Urgent requests**: Immediate attention, fast-track solutions
    
    **Example**:
    Customer: "This is ridiculous! My order was supposed to arrive yesterday and now you're saying it's delayed AGAIN?!"
    
    AI Response: "I completely understand your frustration, and I sincerely apologize for the delay. Let me look into your order immediately and see what we can do to make this right..."
